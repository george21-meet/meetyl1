Caged Apple Puff

Ingredients:
Steps:
  1. Cut the pastry into 9 equal squares
  2. Make 5 equal 1-inch curs on 2 sides on one of the squares
  3. Make 3 vertical 1-inch cuts in between the edge of the uncut side and the 2 slits on the cut sides
  4. Make 2 vertical 1-inch cuts under the top slit and above the bottom slit
  5. Repeat the pattern until you have 6 rows with 3 cuts and 5 rows with slits and 2 cuts
  6. Preheat your oven to 200C(400F)
  7. Take another square, place one of the apple halves in the center of the square. Make sure the apple is peeled and facing up. Brush egg wash on each side of the square surrounding the apple.
  8. Combine the butter, cinnamon, and sugar in a bowl. Brush the apple until its fully coated
  9. Take the other cut square of dough and stretch it over the apple, the cut you made should look like holes in the dough
  10. Pinch the bottom of the dough together until its sealed
  11. Brush with egg wash and sprinke with coarse sugar
  12. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the pastry is golden brown and puffed
